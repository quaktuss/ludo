# Privacy Policy - Ludo Discord Bot

**Last updated:** March 20, 2026

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Discord Data
- **User IDs**: Discord user identifiers for authentication and authorization
- **Server IDs**: Discord server (guild) identifiers to manage per-server configurations
- **Username**: Discord username for logging and display purposes

### 1.2 Bot Usage Data
- **Commands Used**: Which bot commands are executed for functionality and debugging
- **Server Configurations**: Game server settings, resource allocations, and preferences
- **Timestamps**: When actions are performed for logging and troubleshooting

### 1.3 Technical Data
- **Error Logs**: Technical errors for debugging and service improvement
- **Performance Metrics**: Basic usage statistics to optimize the bot's performance

## 2. How We Use Your Information

### 2.1 Core Functionality
- Create and manage game servers as requested
- Authenticate users and enforce permissions
- Store server configurations and preferences
- Provide technical support and troubleshooting

### 2.2 Service Improvement
- Analyze usage patterns to improve bot features
- Debug technical issues and errors
- Monitor performance and reliability

## 3. Information Storage and Security

### 3.1 Data Storage
- Data is stored in AWS DynamoDB with encryption at rest
- Server configurations are stored only as long as needed for functionality
- User data is retained for operational purposes

### 3.2 Security Measures
- All data transmission uses encryption (TLS/SSL)
- Access to data is restricted to necessary systems only
- Regular security audits and updates are performed

## 4. Data Sharing

### 4.1 We Do NOT Share Data With:
- Third-party advertising companies
- Data brokers or marketing companies
- Social media platforms
- Analytics companies for profiling

### 4.2 We May Share Data Only When:
- Required by law or legal process
- Necessary to protect our rights or safety
- With your explicit consent
- With service providers who assist in bot operation (under strict confidentiality)

## 5. Third-Party Services

### 5.1 Discord
- The bot operates on Discord's platform and is subject to Discord's Privacy Policy
- Discord may have access to bot interactions as per their terms

### 5.2 Cloud Infrastructure
- We use AWS services for hosting and data storage
- AWS has their own privacy policies governing their services
- Data is processed in compliance with applicable data protection laws

## 6. Data Retention

- **Active Data**: Server configurations are kept while servers are active
- **Logs**: Error and usage logs are retained for 90 days maximum
- **Inactive Data**: Data from deleted servers is purged within 30 days
- **User Requests**: Data can be deleted upon user request

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request information about data we store about you
- **Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your data
- **Portability**: Request export of your data
- **Objection**: Object to certain data processing activities

## 8. Children's Privacy

The bot is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the information promptly.

## 9. International Data Transfers

Data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

## 10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes through the bot or Discord announcements. Continued use constitutes acceptance of changes.
